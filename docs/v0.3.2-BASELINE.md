## 1. Compiler Ghost Exorcism (−10.3% Regression Fix)

**Root cause:** `#ifdef RDNA2_MATMUL_OPT_V1` compile-time gate caused LLVM to take suboptimal optimization branches for the entire `mul_mat_q` function, even when the runtime gate (`use_experimental`) was `false`.

**Fix:** Removed the `#ifdef` wrapper in `mmq.cuh`. Both experimental (LDS double-buffer) and standard matmul paths are now **always compiled**, dispatched via `getenv("RDNA2_MATMUL_OPT_V1")` at runtime. This eliminates compiler optimization divergence between builds with and without the flag.

### Prefill Recovery (8B Q4_K_M, no turbo/FA)

| State | pp512 | Delta |
|-------|:-----:|:-----:|
| Fork A (regressed) | 1178 ± 1 t/s | −10.3% |
| **Now (after fix)** | **1247 ± 29 t/s** | **−5.1%** |
| Fork B (v0.3.0 target) | 1314 ± 1 t/s | — |

**Result:** Recovered ~70% of the regression (+69 t/s). Remaining ~5% gap attributed to other upstream changes between v0.3.0-stable and current HEAD.

- **ncmoe=8 is insufficient** — 21 GB model with only 8 expert groups on CPU causes heavy GTT spill (±343 t/s, 54% jitter)
- **ncmoe=30 is the sweet spot** — 848.59 ± 34.85 t/s at pp512, clean 4% jitter; VRAM fits within budget
- ncmoe=30 offloads ~37.5% of MoE expert layers to CPU, freeing ~2-3 GB of VRAM
- tg128 at 45 t/s is a significant decode improvement over the dense 27B (27 t/s) — MoE efficiently utilizes GPU compute

## 6. VRAM Budgeting Reference

### Formula
```
V_budget = VRAM_total - OS_headroom - KV_cache
ngl = min(L, floor((V_budget - Scratch) / W_l))
```

### Reference Values for RX 6800 XT (16,384 MiB)

| Model | Size | Layers | W_l (MB) | OS (MiB) | KV@8K (MiB) | Scratch (MiB) | Max -ngl |
|-------|:----:|:------:|:--------:|:--------:|:-----------:|:-------------:|:--------:|
| 8B Q4_K_M | 4.6 GB | 32 | 144 | 1024 | 14 (FA) | 266 | 99 |
| 27B IQ4_XS | 14 GB | ~80 | 162 | 1024 | 156 (FA+Turbo) | 505 | 99 |
| 35B MoE Q4 | 19 GB | ~80 | 162 | 1024 | ~200 (FA+Turbo) | ~500 | 99* |

*With `-ncmoe` to offload MoE experts

### Safety Rules
1. **Always keep ≥1,024 MiB free** (prevents GTT leakage / ±600 t/s jitter)
2. **Flash Attention** reduces KV cache footprint by ~80% at 8K+ context
3. **TurboQuant cache** (turbo4/turbo2) further compresses KV cache with minimal quality loss
4. **Auto-fit** (`-fitt 1024 -fitc N`) handles -ngl calculation automatically
5. **Context costs ~8-10 layers per 8K** of VRAM (without Flash Attention)
